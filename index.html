Time Management Tips

📚 Academic Focus

Use planners and Pomodoro timers to stay consistent with your study schedule.

💼 Part-Time Jobs

Use a calendar to block working hours. Don't overcommit during exam weeks.

🧘 Personal Life

Reserve time weekly for social life, sports, or self-care to avoid burnout.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I manage time with part-time jobs and classes?

A: Use time-blocking techniques and prioritize urgent academic tasks before work shifts. Keep one day fully free if possible.

Q: I'm new to Germany and overwhelmed. What can I do?

A: Talk to student advisors, use our videos, and connect with senior students or forums. You're not alone!
